Pruning

Think of this as fine-tuning a tree by removing redundant branches. We apply the same philosophy to AI models, trimming excess to ensure they're streamlined yet powerful.

Quantization

By fine-tuning the precision of numerical values within our models, we've dramatically shrunk memory demands and augmented processing speeds without compromising accuracy.

Binarization

At its core, binarization is about simplicity, transforming weights and activations into binary states. This not only trims memory use but significantly boosts model speed.

Distillation

Knowledge is passed from a vast, intricate model to its leaner counterpart. The result? Models that are nimble yet do not skimp on accuracy.
